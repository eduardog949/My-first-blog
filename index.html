Hobbies

Immediatley after my break up I thought having hobbies was stupid. I believed that they were a waste of time, a childish past time that only made you ignore other responsibilities in life. Now that somebody time has passed, I have returned to making music for fun and coding. I understand now that sometimes your mind needs to be active in doing something you love, as this is what has helped me to get out of my head. When I work on my hobbies, I can tune out all the negative thoughts and just watch the time pass. Hobbies are not stupid, they are essential for the high paced racing mind.

Yellow

This song gets me everytime. My father used to play it every day after he was diagnosed with prostate cancer. I don't know if he actually knew what the song was saying.. It wasn't until after he passed that I heard the singer singing " Your skin , yeah your skin and bones, turn into something beautiful and you know, you know I love you so, you know I love you so". Did my father resonate with being called skin n bones due to his detoriating body? Did he translate turning into something beautiful as dieing and crossing over to a better place where he would have a brand new body? Was he sublinimally telling me he loved me through the song since he struggled so hard to say those words all his life? It is funny how music can make you rationalize things. Maybe he just liked the melodies of the song.
